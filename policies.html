Policies

Each project is a team project. You are responsible for forming / joining a team. You are more than welcome to switch teams between projects and/or for the final project.

Each team should have 2-3 people. More or fewer requires permission from the instructor.

Projects are due by 11:59:59pm on the noted date. Projects that are late will be heavily penalized; better to submit incomplete than late.

Each project is two weeks long. The final project is four weeks long (more details will be released later in the semester).

The UGA Academic Honesty Policy is the final word on these matters. Lack of knowledge of these policies is not sufficient justification for violations. If in doubt, ask me.
